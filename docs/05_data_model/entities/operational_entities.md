# Operational & Platform Entities

**Date:** April 2026  
**Status:** Verified against code

---

These entities support platform operations, monitoring, and advanced financial features. They were added in later phases and may not appear in earlier documentation.

## OperationalInsight

**File:** `CephasOps.Domain/Operations/Entities/OperationalInsight.cs`

Platform-level operational insights generated by background analysis jobs.

---

## MigrationAudit

**File:** `CephasOps.Domain/Operations/Entities/MigrationAudit.cs`

Tracks database migration execution history for operational monitoring.

---

## ExternalIdempotencyRecord

**File:** `CephasOps.Domain/Common/ExternalIdempotencyRecord.cs`

Prevents duplicate processing of external events/webhooks. Stores a unique key per external operation to ensure at-most-once semantics.

---

## OrderFinancialAlert

**File:** `CephasOps.Domain/Orders/Entities/OrderFinancialAlert.cs`  
**Base Class:** `CompanyScopedEntity`

Flags orders with financial anomalies (cost overruns, pricing mismatches, margin alerts).

---

## BaseWorkRate / GponSiJobRate

**Files:** `CephasOps.Domain/Rates/Entities/BaseWorkRate.cs`, `GponSiJobRate.cs`

Rate engine entities for GPON service installer job pricing. Support versioned pricing with effective date ranges.

---

## Inventory Extensions

### LedgerBalanceCache

**File:** `CephasOps.Domain/Inventory/Entities/LedgerBalanceCache.cs`

Cached ledger balance for performance — avoids recalculating from full ledger history on every query.

### StockByLocationSnapshot

**File:** `CephasOps.Domain/Inventory/Entities/StockByLocationSnapshot.cs`

Point-in-time snapshot of stock levels per location. Used for reporting and reconciliation.

### MaterialPartner

**File:** `CephasOps.Domain/Inventory/Entities/MaterialPartner.cs`

Links materials to specific partners with partner-specific metadata (partner SKU codes, pricing).
